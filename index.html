The Mechanical Engineering Department is the cornerstone of innovation and precision
in the world of engineering. With a rich history dating back to the Industrial Revolution,
it continues to play a pivotal role in designing, analyzing, and creating mechanical systems
that drive modern technology. Our department is home to a diverse and passionate community
of engineers who explore cutting-edge technologies, from robotics and aerospace to sustainable
energy solutions. Join us on a journey of discovery and invention that shapes the future of
mechanical engineering.

The college is situated in a prime location in the Cooch Behar District. The college, being well connected from any part of the Cooch Behar city, enables the student's easy access.
